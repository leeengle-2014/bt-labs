How does it work?

Pure p2p

Sync uses p2p protocol for synchronization. This protocol is very efective for transferring large files across multiple devices, which is successfully proved by applications like uTorrent and BitTorrent. The data is transferred in pieces from each of the syncing devices, and Sync chooses the optimal algorithm of connection to make sure you have maximum download and upload speed during the process.

Direct transfers

All the cloud services use 3rd party servers for storing or transferring yoru data. We don't. Your data is kept on your devices and transfers directly among them. Absence of intermediate links of synchronization gives you not only greater security, but also top speed and no file size limits.

Guaranteed connection

We will always connect your devices. It is achieved by use of such native p2p techniques as UDP, NAT traversal, UPnP port mapping and others. If your devices are in a local network, Sync will connect them directly in LAN synchronize them at the maximum speed of the network connection.

Privacy in mind

Sync was designed with privacy in mind. ALl information belongs to you. Only you have full control of it. You don't have to share any information with us or register before you start using our app. All you need to do is generate a Secret for a folder and enter it on all the devices you want to sync with. This Secret is used to connect all your devices directly.

Encrypted p2p

We ensure that your information is safe during transit from one device to another. It is done by encryptiong all the traffic with AES 256 using a private key created on the base of your Secret - a random key that is different for every folder. Nobody without this key can intercept or alter your data.

No storage server

It is our priority to make sure nobody has unauthorized access to your media and documents. That's why we don't keep any of it on 3rd-party servers. All the files are stored only on your own trusted devices, controlled and managed by you.
